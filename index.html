Detailed Mixtures & Construction Guide

Black Powder Mixtures

1. Maximum Power

  • Potassium Nitrate: 77%
  • Charcoal: 13%
  • Sulfur: 10%
  • Fast ignition, high energy, short burn.

2. Longer Burn

  • Potassium Nitrate: 70%
  • Charcoal: 20%
  • Sulfur: 10%
  • Moderate ignition, steady sparks.

Sugar Rocket Fuel Mixtures

1. Maximum Power

  • Potassium Nitrate: 70%
  • Sugar: 25%
  • Aluminum Powder: 5%
  • High thrust, short burn duration.

2. Longer Burn

  • Potassium Nitrate: 60%
  • Sugar: 40%
  • Easier ignition, slower, longer-lasting burn.

Flash Powder Mixtures

1. Maximum Power (Rapid Ignition, Loudest Report)

  • Potassium Perchlorate: 70%
  • Aluminum Powder (fine mesh): 30%
  • Extremely bright flash, loud explosion.

2. Longer Burn (Slightly Slower Flash)

  • Potassium Nitrate: 55%
  • Aluminum Powder: 35%
  • Sulfur: 10%
  • Bright flash with slightly longer burn.

Highly sensitive and dangerous—handle with extreme caution.

Smoke Grenade Mixtures

1. Maximum Smoke Volume

  • Potassium Nitrate: 55%
  • Sugar: 45%
  • Baking soda (~2%) optional for denser smoke.

2. Longer Smoke Duration

  • Potassium Nitrate: 65%
  • Sugar: 35%
  • Produces steady, prolonged smoke.

Safety Precautions

  • Always test small quantities first.
  • Store materials safely, away from ignition sources.
  • Don't be irresponsible—handle all materials carefully.

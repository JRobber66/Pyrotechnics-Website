Detailed Mixtures & Construction Guide

Black Powder Mixtures

1. Maximum Power

  • Potassium Nitrate: 77%
  • Charcoal:13%
  • Sulfur:10%
  • Fast ignition, high energy, short burn.

2. Longer Burn

  • Potassium Nitrate:70%
  • Charcoal:20%
  • Sulfur:10%
  • Moderate ignition, steady sparks.
  • Potassium Nitrate:60%
  • Sugar:40%
  • Optional: Baking soda (2%) for denser smoke.

Strobe Firework Mixtures

  • Potassium Nitrate/Perchlorate:50%
  • Sulfur:30%
  • Fine Magnesium/Aluminum:20%

Sugar Rocket Fuel Mixtures

1. Maximum Power

  • Potassium Nitrate:70%
  • Sugar:25%
  • Aluminum Powder:5%

2. Longer Burn

  • Potassium Nitrate:60%
  • Sugar:40%

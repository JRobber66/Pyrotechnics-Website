Detailed Mixtures & Construction Guide

Black Powder Mixtures

1. Maximum Power

  • Potassium Nitrate: 77%
  • Charcoal: 13%
  • Sulfur: 10%
  • Fast ignition, high energy, short burn.

2. Longer Burn

  • Potassium Nitrate: 70%
  • Charcoal: 20%
  • Sulfur: 10%
  • Moderate ignition, steady sparks.
  • Potassium Nitrate: 60%
  • Sugar: 40%
  • Optional: Baking soda (2%) to slow reaction for denser smoke.

Strobe Firework Mixtures

  • Potassium Nitrate or Perchlorate: 50%
  • Sulfur: 30%
  • Fine Magnesium or Aluminum Powder: 20%
  • Pulsing strobe effect due to controlled combustion.

Sugar Rocket Fuel Mixtures

1. Maximum Power

  • Potassium Nitrate: 70%
  • Sugar: 25%
  • Aluminum Powder: 5%
  • High thrust, short burn duration.

2. Longer Burn

  • Potassium Nitrate: 60%
  • Sugar: 40%
  • Easier ignition, slower, longer-lasting burn.

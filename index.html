Detailed Mixtures & Construction Guide

Black Powder Mixtures

1. Maximum Power

  • Potassium Nitrate: 77%
  • Charcoal: 13%
  • Sulfur: 10%
  • Fast ignition, high energy, short burn.

2. Longer Burn

  • Potassium Nitrate: 70%
  • Charcoal: 20%
  • Sulfur: 10%
  • Moderate ignition, steady sparks.

Smoke Bomb Mixtures

1. Maximum Smoke Output

  • Potassium Nitrate: 55%
  • Sugar: 45%
  • Optional: Baking soda (2%) to slow reaction for denser smoke.

2. Longer Burn Time

  • Potassium Nitrate: 65%
  • Sugar: 35%
  • Burns slower, producing steady smoke.

Strobe Firework Mixtures

  • Potassium Nitrate or Potassium Perchlorate: ~50%
  • Sulfur: ~30%
  • Fine Magnesium or Aluminum Powder: ~20%
  • Produces a pulsing strobe effect due to controlled combustion.
  • Best used in a small cardboard tube with a fuse or igniter.

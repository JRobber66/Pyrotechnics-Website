Sleeping in the Nether: What Could Go Wrong?

So you’re deep in the Nether, the lava's flowing, piglins are vibing, and you’re thinking: “Wow, I could really use a nap.”

We’ve all been there. But let us stop you right now. If you try to sleep in the Nether, you’re not getting rest. You’re getting a one-way ticket to BOOMsville.

But Why Though?

Minecraft lore says you can’t dream in hell. So instead of sleeping, the bed self-destructs. Think of it as the bed flipping YOU off.

Explosion Facts

  • Damage radius: big enough to erase your hopes and items
  • Block destruction: absolutely, yes
  • Chance of survival: depends on how recently you angered the RNG gods

What to Do Instead

Need a spawn point? Use a Respawn Anchor, a.k.a. the “Totally Not A Bed” bed. Just charge it with glowstone and don't ask questions.

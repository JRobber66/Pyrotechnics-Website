Minecart Speed Tests

In the name of science (and chaos), we asked the age-old question: how fast can a minecart go when a pig is inside?

Methodology

Our high-tech lab (a flat world with rails and too much free time) was used to test various minecart setups. The pig was incentivized with carrots and existential dread.

Test Conditions

  • Standard rail
  • Powered rail every 5 blocks
  • Saddle-equipped pig (for vibes)
  • Measured in Blocks Per Second (BPS)

Speed Test Results

Test Setup Speed (BPS) Pig Mood
Empty Minecart 8 BPS Non-existent
Minecart with Player 8 BPS Paranoia
Minecart with Pig 8 BPS Confused but polite
Minecart with Pig + Rocket Boost ??? Left the simulation

Conclusion

It turns out pigs in minecarts are just as fast as players — truly the great equalizer. However, adding rocket propulsion results in a reality-breaking event best described as “oink-meets-hyperspace.”

Bonus Observation

If you name the pig “Speedy Boi” and give it sunglasses (resource pack required), it goes 15% faster. Probably.
